UDPL Summer Family Picnic

 

The UDPL is holding a Summer Family Picnic on Saturday, May 29th from 11 a.m. to 2 p.m. at the park just south of the Orem City Recreation center. The park is adjacent to a fully equipped playground for the kids.

 

This is a chance for all of the UDPL families (as well as those of our other fellow shooters) to come together and do something besides make loud noises with firearms. I'm sure there will be plenty of "gun talk", but the real purpose is to have a fun day getting acquainted with each other and “meet the family”.

 

We are having hamburgers, hotdogs and all the other fun foods that go with them. The Club will furnish the entire meal, so just come, eat and enjoy each others company.

 

Costs for the meal are:

    - $10.00 per family for UDPL members

     - $15.00 per family for Non-UDPL members

 

Kent Redford will be in charge of the arrangements, and we would like to get some idea how many will be attending. Please click on Kent’s E-mail link and let him know if you will be coming and how many will be in your family. Also, if you could help with the event, I’m sure he would appreciate the offer.

 

Please reply A.S.A.P., but no later than Friday, May 21st.

 

See you there!!!
