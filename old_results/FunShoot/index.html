Rangemasters of Utah Handgun "Fun" Shoot Schedule & Results

(Note:  The Rangemasters Handgun "Fun" Shoot matches ARE NOT UDPL events.
As a service to local shooters, the UDPL posts these match schedules and results.)
 

The Rangemasters Handgun "Fun" Shoot matches
 are held on the 1st Wednesday and 3rd Tuesday of each month
at 6:30 p.m. at the Range Masters of Utah indoor shooting range in Springville.

Shooters who have not participated in a previous "Fun" Shoot must
attend the New Shooter Orientation held at 6:45 p.m.
 

Match Fees:

  • 1 Scoresheet for $13.00 (Normal Individual Price including tax)

  • 2 Scoresheets for $20.00 (If the same individual wants to shoot a 2nd time – not to be shared between two shooters)

  • $2.00 off per box of handgun ammo (excluding .22 ammo)

Please be on time.

No "new shooters" will be allowed after 6:45 p.m.

 No experienced shooters will be allowed after 7:30 p.m.

Only Strong-Side Holsters are allowed.

At least 3 Mags/Speedloaders are recommended.

Bullets used must be jacketed, plated or frangible - no totally-lead ammo.
