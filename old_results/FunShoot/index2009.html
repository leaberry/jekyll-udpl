Range Masters of Utah "Fun" Shoot Schedule & Results

In 2009, the Range Masters "Fun" Shoot handgun matches
 will be held on the 1st Wednesday and 3rd Tuesday of each month
at 6:30 p.m. at the Range Masters of Utah indoor shooting range in Springville.
 Match Fee:  $12.00 + tax ($12.75)
Please be on time.
No "new shooters" will be allowed after 6:45 p.m.
 No experienced shooters will be allowed after 7:30 p.m.
Only Strong-Side Holsters are allowed.
At least 3 Mags/Speedloaders are recommended.

(Click on logo for directions, etc.)


(Note:  The Range Masters "Fun" Shoot matches are not UDPL events.
As a service to Utah County shooters, the UDPL posts this match schedule and results.)
