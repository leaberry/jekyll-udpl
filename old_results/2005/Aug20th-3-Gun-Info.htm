UDPL 3-Gun Match Information (Amended 8/10/05)–

 

Saturday, August 20th

 

Sign in = 8 a.m.

 

New Shooters Orientation = 8:30 a.m.

 

Minimum Rounds:

  Rifle        = 76

  Pistol      = 91

  Birdshot = 50

  Slugs      = 3

    Bring extra of everything!

 

Particulars:

  • There are 8 stages that are all Vickers count.
  • There are 3 stages that we will be using all 3 guns.

 

  There are three things that shooters need to be aware of:

1.    We will be using one of our new "pigeon flippers". This is a lever connected to a falling steel target. When the falling target hits this lever, a clay pigeon will fly straight up about 25 feet. You will be expected to hit the bird before it falls to the ground.

2.    We will also be shooting at a gallon milk jug full of water at about 65 yds with SLUGS. Are you sighted in?

3.    We will be shooting at clay pigeons with rifle @ 25 yds away. Break 'em or chip 'em.

 

Friday afternoon set up at the FARM approx. 3 p.m.  Any help in setting up would be greatly appreciated.
