September 24th Team Rifle/Shotgun Side Match Information -

 

Teams:

  2-man; 1 rifle, 1 shotgun

 

Stages:

  1 long stage

 

Round Count:

  Rifle = 50 (bring extra)

  Shotgun = 25 (bring extra)

 

Format:

  Team will shoot together at the same time, one shooter on rifle, one on shotgun

 

Misc:

  Team will be required to move, communicate and coordinate their actions
