Notes:  Due to the number of shooters that participated in the 2003 competition, three separate nights were required to allow all competitors to shoot. As a result, the organizers thought it appropriate to award Gold, Silver and Bonze medals for each night of the competition.

Also, because the members of the Utah Defensive Pistol League (UDPL) volunteered to act as Safety Officers, they were allowed to compete and received Gold, Silver and Bronze medals for their overall competition placing.
