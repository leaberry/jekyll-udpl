UDPL .22 LR Only Match Information –

The July 17th UDPL Match will be a .22 Long Rifle Only Match consisting of the following:

 

Semi-auto, mag-fed firearms recommended, with a minimum of four 10-round mags. Revolvers with speed loaders may also be used.

 

Round Count: 250-300

 

Stages: 8-10

 

Expect stages with as many as 30 rounds and have equipment suitable to be able to complete the course of fire.

 

Divisions:

Pistol w/Iron Sights (PIS) – Single Pistol or Revolver with Iron Sights Only

Pistol w/Optical Sight (POS) – Single Pistol or Revolver with an Optical Sight

Rifle w/Iron Sights (RIS) – Single Rifle with Iron Sights Only

Rifle w/Optical Sight (ROS) – Single Rifle with an Optical Sight

OPEN – Anything goes

 

Pistol and Rifle division participants must use the same gun in the same configuration for the entire match.

 

OPEN division participants can use different firearms and configurations for different stages.
