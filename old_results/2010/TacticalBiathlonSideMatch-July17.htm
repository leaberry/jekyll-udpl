Tactical Biathlon Side Match in July –
There are plans for a Tactical Biathlon Side Match following the 22 LR Match on July 17, 2010. Note:  This is a schedule change from the normal 4th Saturday.

Ari Theodore is developing a one stage competition that combines walking or running, rifle shooting (any caliber), and pistol shooting (any caliber). It also has time penalties for missed shots.

Details –

  • Locations: The Gulch & City Limits with the running course out to the Back Forty.
  • Distance: .75 – 1.0 mile
  • Start:  At the Global one entrance gate.
  • Divisions: .22 or Standard (anything larger than .22 cal)
  • Firearms Req: Pistol and Rifle (if one of the firearms is larger than .22 cal, you are in the Standard Div.)
  • Round Count: 16 rifle, 16 pistol (max of 30rds of rifle and 35 pistol).
  • Misc: Shooter must utilize a secure strong side holster for pistol (must be able to hold pistol while shooter is moving at full speed).  Shooter can utilize a sling for the rifle.  *Please be aware that this is an event where the shooter must travel on uneven terrain carrying weapons in a hot dry climate for up to one mile and shooting from both standing and prone.
  • Questions: refer to Ari Theodore: atwolf@hotmail.com
