UDPL Rifle/Carbine Match Information

 

Rifle/Carbine Match Divisions -

IRON    =  Iron Sights Only

OPT      =  Optical sights

OPEN    =  For those who wish to change rifles and/or uppers during the course of the match. Of course an optic or more than one optic is allowed.

NHP      =  Non-High Power includes pistol caliber and .22 LR rifle/carbines

 

Use of Chamber Safety Flags will be required for this match. If you do not have these, they will be available for purchase at the match during registration for $1.00 each.
