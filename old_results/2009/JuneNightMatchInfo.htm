June UDPL Night Match Information –

The June 27, 2009 UDPL Night Match will be a Handgun/IDPA match.  We will shoot 6 defensive stages with 80 rounds minimum required.

Schedule:

  • 2:00 p.m. - Set Up
  • 7:00 p.m. - Sign In and casual steel shooting ‘till 8:40
  • 9:00 p.m. - Shooters Meeting
  • 9:30 p.m. - Hammer Down

 

Requirements:

  • No new shooters.  Please attend one daytime UDPL match before the night match.  The final UDPL match to be held before the Night Match is the 3-Gun Match on 6/20.  If you think you deserve a special exemption, contact Steve Koski at least two days before the match at koski88@hotmail.com.
  • A hand held flashlight is required for the match.  You can share one with a buddy.  Nearly any hand held light will work.  It doesn’t have to be tacti-cool.
  • If you have an LED head lamp or a less intense “non-tactical” flashlight, please bring it too.  It will help with scoring and taping.
  • We will have two “nightstand start” stages where you can use any gun mounted light/laser setup that you wish, or a hand held flashlight.  I suggest you start these stages with your actual nightstand setup.
  • For the rest of the match, you may only use a gun mounted light/laser if it fits completely in your strong side hip holster.  This will also bump you to a special “Enhanced” scoring division.
  • Some folks like to shoot a few night match stages with carry ammo.  Probably a good idea.
  • The entire match will be freestyle!  No strong hand/weak hand requirement.
